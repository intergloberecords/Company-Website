Interglobe manifesto

Founded by a collective of producers and DJs, Interglobe Records is a vessel for music without borders. We believe in cross‑pollination — from Sao Paulo basslines to Mongolian overtone chants, everything is material.

Our roster includes both emerging and established acts. We offer full label services, vinyl pressings, and a global digital distribution network. Every release is treated as a piece of art.
